Welcome

This is a simple tool to convert your quiz questions into GIFT format for Moodle. Use the navigation above to explore its features.

Instructions

Follow these steps to use the tool:

  1. Go to the "Converter" section.
  2. Paste your questions into the text area.
  3. Click "Convert to GIFT" to generate the output.
  4. Download the GIFT file and upload it to Moodle.
